Shrimp and Artichoke Pasta

Yield: 4 servings
Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 10 minutes
Total Time: 20 minutes

Super easy, super bougie-looking Costco meal.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz frozen shrimp, cooked, tail off (Kirkland)
  • 1 cup Artichoke Lemon Pesto (Cibo Naturals)
  • 12 oz gemelli pasta (Garofalo Organic Pasta)

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta.
  2. Warm the shrimp.
  3. Toss in the pasta and sauce.

Notes

Bam. Bougie meal.
