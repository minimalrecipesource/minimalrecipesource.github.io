Chickie-Pea-Chickpea Stir Fry

Yield: 4 servings
Prep Time: 5 minutes
Cook Time: 20 minutes
Total Time: 25 minutes

Indian-inspired stir fry of chicken, peas, and chickpeas.

Ingredients

Aromatics

  • 1 large onion
  • 1 tbsp garlic minced

Spices

  • 1.5 x Keema Spice Blend

Meats, veggies, etc…

  • 1 ½ lbs ground chicken (or turkey, or beef, or lamb)
  • 15 oz green peas canned or frozen
  • 15 oz garbanzo beans canned

Toppings

  • ¼ cup fresh coriander leaves (cilantro)

  1. See the About page

Notes

  • naan
  • roti
