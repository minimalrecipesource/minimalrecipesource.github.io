Kofta

Yield: 1 spice blend
Prep Time: 5 minutes
Total Time: 5 minutes

Persian Spice Blend

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • ½ tsp ground allspice
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • ⅛ tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 ½ tsp kosher salt

Instructions

  1. Mix ingredients
  2. Put on food
