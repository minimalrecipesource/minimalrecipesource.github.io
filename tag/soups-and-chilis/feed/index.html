Lebanese Lamb Stew

Prep Time: 30 minutes
Cook Time: 30 minutes
Total Time: 1 hour

Lamb stew with Swiss chard and Lebanese spices.

Ingredients

Aromatics

  • 1/4 cup garlic minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger minced
  • 8 oz carrots sliced
  • 1 bunch swiss chard stems (sliced, like you would celery)

Spice Blend

  • 1 x Lebanese Seven Spice Blend

Meat

  • 2 lbs ground lamb (1 lb is also fine if you like things a bit less meaty)

Vegetables and such

  • 10 oz sweet potato, diced
  • 1 bunch swiss chard (leaves, torn)
  • ½ lb red lentils (dry)
  • ¾ cup fresh coriander leaves (cilantro), chopped

Liquids

  • 1 lemon juice
  • 32 oz chicken stock (for cooking lentils)
  • 32 oz chicken stock (for making this an actual soup)

Instructions

Check out the About page.

Notes

Cook the lentils in the chicken broth first. Then do the usual aromatics -> spices -> meats -> veggies -> liquid. Add the lentils back in with the veggies.

Pumpkin Chili

Yield: 10 servings
Prep Time: 30 minutes
Cook Time: 40 minutes
Total Time: 1 hour 10 minutes

Ingredients

Aromatics

  • 1 onion
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 2 tbsp minced ginger

Spice Blend

  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 ½ tsp cinnamon
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 whole clove
  • ⅛ tsp cayenne

Meat

  • 1 lb ground beef

Vegetables and Stuff

  • 7 oz carrots, sliced
  • 4 oz can of mild green chilis
  • 8 oz baby bella mushrooms
  • 15 oz sweet corn
  • 15 oz pureed pumpkin
  • 15 oz black beans
  • 28 oz diced tomatoes

Liquid

  • Chicken broth

Instructions

Check out the About page

Notes

I used about 64 oz (8 cups) of chicken broth.
